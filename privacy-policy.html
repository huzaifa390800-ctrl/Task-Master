Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TaskMaster collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address when you create an account.
  • Task data and usage information to improve our services.
  • Cookies and analytics data to monitor website performance and usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Communicate important updates and notifications.
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience.
  • Protect against fraud and ensure security.

3. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Cookies

TaskMaster uses cookies to improve user experience, track website usage, and customize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may share information with trusted service providers to operate our services effectively, under confidentiality agreements.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to review this page regularly for any changes. Continued use of TaskMaster constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@taskmaster.com.
